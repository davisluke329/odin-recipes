Perfect Toast

Perfect Toast Every Time!

This toast is good for any occasion at any time.

Ingredients

  • Bread:Whatever your favorite bread is.
  • Butter:1 tbsp per piece of toast.
  • Toppings:This is optional. You can choose to add jelly, jam, or anything else your heart desires to put on toast.

Steps

  1. Heat the oven.Heat the oven by putting it on a high broil.
  2. Butter the bread.Slice the butter into 1/2 tbsp pieces and place them evenly spaced on the bread.
  3. Put bread in the oven.Put the bread in the oven. Leave it in for up to 2 minutes. Any longer and you will burn the bread.
  4. Enjoy!Remove the toast from the oven and enjoy.
