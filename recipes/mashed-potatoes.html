Mashed Potatoes

Caution: Extremely Tasty!

Mashed potatoes are a perfect side dish to go with any meal. This recipe shows you how to create the best side dish of all time.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes:6-8 peeled and diced. The type of potatoes is your preferece. I prefer Idaho Potatoes or Russet Potatoes
  • Coarse Sea Salt:4 tbsp
  • Ground Black Pepper:2 tbsp
  • Sour Cream:1 tbsp
  • Milk:1/4 cup. You can use 2% or whole milk.
  • Butter1-1/2 sticks

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes.Bring water to a boil and add the peeled and diced potatoes to the water. You shouldn't need more than 8 cups of water. Boil potatoes until they are soft. You can check the softness by taking a piece out and crushing it with a fork.
  2. Mash potatoes.Once the potatoes are soft, drain the water and place the potatoes in a large wooden or plastic bowl. Using an electric mixer or a hand masher, start mashing the potatoes until they start to blend together and do not contain chunks.
  3. Add the milk.Slowly add milk to the potatoes while you continue to mash them. This will start the fluff the potatoes.
  4. Add the butter. Place a stick of butter in the bowl and continue to mash the potatoes until the butter has completely melted.
  5. Add sour cream. Add the sour cream to the bowl and mix evenly.
  6. Top with salt and pepper.Top with salt and pepper and your potatoes are ready to serve!
